Anah Shrine NASCAR Weekend 2013
3rd Annual – June 28th & 29th

The Anah Shrine NASCAR Unit and Timberland Acres RV Park are teaming up to have a fun packed family weekend and to raise money for the Anah Shrine Children’s Hospital. Save the dates of Friday, June 28th  and Saturday, 29th to be a part of the festivities. The RV Park will be decorated in NASCAR memorabilia in honor of the Anah Shrine NASCAR Unit who will be co-hosting this event. Activities planned for the weekend include a pancake breakfast, hay rides, parade, family games, Chinese and silent auctions and watching the Saturday night race on the big screen.

Saturday events are open to the public, however space is limited so advanced reservations are suggested. If you would like more information, please call Timberland Acres RV Park at (207) 667-3600. We hope to see you there!

All money raised this weekend is to benefit the Shrine Children’s Hospital
