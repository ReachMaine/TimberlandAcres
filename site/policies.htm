the fine print

Fine print sound scary? Not really. At Timberland Acres RV Park, we understand how important it is that your time spent with us is enjoyable and memorable. To that end, we have a few rules and policies detailed here and more on our park guide maps to ensure the comfort, safety and enjoyment of ALL our guests.

Registration

Check-in time is any time after noon
Check-out time is 11:00 AM
Reservations will be held until 6:00 PM
Visitors must register at the office
Day visitor charges apply
Visitors must leave by 9:30 PM

Quiet hours

10:00 PM to 7:00 AM
No radios outside after 10:00 PM

Swimming Pool

NO LIFEGUARD Swim at your own risk
Children 16 and under must be accompanied by an adult


Timberland Acres — Where the friendly people meet!

Other Amenities

Wood and ice available at the office
Propane sold on site
WiFi connected! Wireless Internet available free throughout the campground, pavilion and in our recreation room
RV supplies available at the office
Fresh Maine Blueberries available in August
Haven's Candies available at the office

Fires

Allowed in fire rings only
Do not move fire rings

Pets

Proof of rabies vaccination required
Allowed only on a leash and must be attended at all times

Rates

Based on a family of four (4)
Additional per person charge for more than four (4) people
Monthly and seasonal rates available

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made 7 days in advance to receive a refund.
One night charge will apply for cancellations made less than 7 days in advance.
Holiday weekend reservations must be paid in full at time of reservation. No refunds for holiday weekends to include Memorial Day, 4th of July and Labor Day.

If you would like more information about Timberland Acres RV Park or would like information about availability, please send us a message. We'd love to hear from you.
