Shrine NASCAR Weekend 2012
July 6th and 7th

See our jamb packed schedule!

The Shrine NASCAR Unit and Timberland Acres RV Park are teaming up to have a fun packed family weekend and to raise money for the Shrine Children’s Hospital. Save the dates of Friday, July 6th and Saturday, July 7th to be a part of the festivities. The RV Park will be decorated in NASCAR memorabilia in honor of the Shrine NASCAR Unit who will be co-hosting this event. Activities planned for the weekend include a pancake breakfast, hay rides, parade, family games, Chinese and silent auctions and watching the Saturday night race on the big screen.

Saturday events are open to the public, however space is limited so advanced reservations are suggested. If you would like more information, please call Timberland Acres RV Park at (207) 667-3600. We hope to see you there!
