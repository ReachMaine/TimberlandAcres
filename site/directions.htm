Directions Coming from the South

  • Follow Interstate 95 North
     

  • Take 295 N around Portland to 95 N (connects outside Augusta/Gardiner area)
     

  • Follow 95 N to Bangor area
     

  • Take exit 182A to 395 (goes around Bangor/Brewer) to exit 6 which is 1A to Ellsworth
     

  • In Ellsworth (approximately 24 miles) 1A meets Routes 3 and 1. Following Route 3 East (also 1 North) toward Bar Harbor and Acadia National Park (stay in Right hand lane and go straight through all lights).
     

  • At McDonald’s light continue straight on Route 3 – you will be on a one-way road going up a hill – stay right and continue straight through 3 more lights.
     

  • We will be about ¾ mile on your right just after the Subaru car dealership. You will see a large red/white sign with our name and before that a smaller sign. Our main entrance is at the smaller sign. Our office is the white building with red awnings.
